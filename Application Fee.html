Application Fee


We want to give all the young adults out there a chance to see the world. So, our service is completely FREE. In return, we just hope that you help us share the love and tell your friends about Ezkie. PayPal charges a 3% + $0.30 fee per transaction. We split this cost equally between the Guest and the Host to make things as fair as possible.

Ezkie collects a 1.5% + $0.15 fee from Guests everytime a reservation is booked to cover the cost of processing payments.
