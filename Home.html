Welcome to Join Us!


We are convinced that the future of our generation lies in our ability to engage in dialogues with people across different regions, cultures, and beliefs. We believe all young adults should live around the world, go out of their comfort zone, and cultivate their cultural curiosity.
We seek to foster cultural, social, and intellectual relationships across the globe by changing the way university students and young professionals travel, live and connect abroad.
We created a secure peer-to-peer home-sharing platform for rooms to rent in houses shared with other students and young professionals to make it easy, safe, and affordable to find an awesome home and housemates who share the same interests and lifestyle anywhere in the world.
We seek to change the housing game and give all young adults the chance the live around the world by building a curated network of universities and corporations where students and young professionals can let their place to each other without the headache of strict long-term leases, high agency fees, or massive deposits.
Now that it’s easy to find your next home abroad, what are you waiting for? It’s time to go overseas and embark on a new adventure!
